CATEGORY CREATORS

THE ORIGINATORS.

WE BROUGHT TRUCK ART TO RUGBY. 2019. PAKISTAN. THE WORLD.

Other brands sell you a clean kit in 3 colors.

We invented a category in 2019: cultural heritage rugby kits. Pakistani truck art — bold, intricate, unmistakable — on a match-day jersey for the first time in any sport. We field-tested it at Dubai 7s the same year.

Then we went further. We fused Russian Khokhloma folk art with Pakistani truck art — the only kit brand in the world building cross-cultural design as a system, not a one-off. Three colorways. Multiple silhouettes. Bridging two nations on a single jersey.

Others followed. The original still stands.
